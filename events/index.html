How Nonprofit EDs Nurture a "Media Capture Culture" in their Organizations

Event Details:

Getting attention from funders, donors, and volunteers means creating a constant stream of media across multiple channels. To be successful, your marketing and communications team needs support from the entire organization. Find out how EDs are providing that support by creating a culture of media capture and sharing.

Hosted by: Joey Azoulai

Joey is a Content Strategist and Video Producer based in New York City. He is the founder of **Impact Video Services**, a creative agency dedicated to making high-quality video production accessible to nonprofits of all sizes.

Previously, Joey served as Director of Media & Content at digital agency Studion, where he led projects for clients such as Harvard Law, Bridgespan, Microsoft, and Goodwill. His work has consistently focused on helping mission-driven organizations engage audiences, secure funding, and demonstrate impact.

Joey’s career spans television and film production, classroom teaching, and leading a media team that developed innovative digital programs to support workforce development and education. He also teaches media production and technology at New York University and Columbia University’s Teachers College, where he mentors emerging professionals in the field.
